About Smriti

The Elegant Southpaw

Born on July 18, 1996, in Mumbai, Smriti Mandhana is an Indian cricketer who plays for the Indian women's national team. Known for her elegant stroke play and leadership qualities, she has established herself as one of the premier batswomen in international cricket.

At just 9 years old, Smriti was selected in the Maharashtra Under-15 team. At 11, she was picked for the Maharashtra Under-19 team. Her international debut came in 2013 against Bangladesh, and she hasn't looked back since.

Beyond her cricketing abilities, Smriti is known for her calm demeanor, humility, and dedication to promoting women's cricket globally.
